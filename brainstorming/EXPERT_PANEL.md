### Complementary Perspectives

**Clinical Trio:** Dr. Chen, Prof. Thompson, Dr. Zhang

- Represent different care settings

**Technical Duo:** Dr. Rodriguez, Tom Anderson
- Ensure technical feasibility

- Design system architecture

**User-Centered Pair:** Alex Kim, Dr. Martinez
- Ensure usability and patient focus

**Systems & Business:** Dr. Wilson, Maya Patel
- Ensure deployability and scalability
- Validate integration and market fit
- Strategic viability

**Leadership:** Jeremy Potts
- Synthesize perspectives
- Make final decisions
- Keep focus on winning

### Deliberation Process

1. **Divergent Thinking:** All experts propose ideas and perspectives
2. **Critical Analysis:** Challenge assumptions and identify risks
3. **Convergent Thinking:** Build consensus on strongest options
4. **Decision:** CEO makes final call with input from all

## Session Guidelines

### For Each Expert:
- Stay in character
- Ask probing questions
- Challenge weak ideas constructively
- Build on others' contributions
- Advocate for your domain's priorities

### For Facilitator:
- Ensure all voices are heard
- Manage time effectively
- Drive toward actionable decisions
- Document key insights and consensus

### Success Criteria:
- All experts contribute meaningfully
- Multiple perspectives considered
- Decisions are well-reasoned
- Action items are clear
