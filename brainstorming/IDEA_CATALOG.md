### 1. RadAssist Pro (Hybrid Radiology Assistant) ⭐ LEAD CONCEPT

**Description:** Comprehensive AI radiology assistant that combines 2D chest X-ray interpretation, 3D CT/MRI volumetric analysis, and longitudinal temporal comparison with automated FHIR-compliant report generation.

**Unique Value Proposition:**
- Only tool leveraging ALL three MedGemma 1.5 unique capabilities
- Addresses highest-volume radiology use case (chest imaging)
- Production-ready architecture with HIPAA compliance

**Technical Components:**

- Longitudinal comparison (competitive moat)
